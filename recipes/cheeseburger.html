Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a classic American indulgence that features a juicy beef patty nestled between toasted buns. The patty, seasoned to perfection and grilled to a mouthwatering char, is topped with melted cheddar cheese that oozes with each bite. Crisp lettuce and slices of ripe tomato add freshness and crunch, while a spread of tangy ketchup and creamy mayonnaise enhances the savory flavors. Pickles provide a zesty kick, balancing the richness of the cheese and beef. This iconic dish is not just a meal but a culinary delight that combines comforting textures and bold flavors, offering a taste of nostalgia with every satisfying bite.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80% lean)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles (sliced)
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Begin by dividing the ground beef into equal portions and shaping them into patties. Season each patty generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the Grill or Pan: Heat a grill or large skillet over medium-high heat. Ensure the grill or skillet is well-oiled to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook the Patties: Place the seasoned patties onto the hot grill or skillet. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, adjusting for desired doneness from medium-rare to well-done.
  4. Toast the Buns: While the patties cook, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until they are golden brown.
  5. Melt the Cheese: During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt slightly.
  6. Assemble the Cheeseburgers: Spread a layer of mayonnaise on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Add a lettuce leaf and tomato slices on top of the mayo.
  7. Add the Patty and Toppings: Place the cooked cheeseburger patty on top of the tomatoes. Add a few slices of pickles on top of the patty.
  8. Finish and Serve: Spread ketchup on the inside of the top half of the toasted bun. Place it on top of the pickles to complete the cheeseburger.
  9. Serve Immediately: Serve the cheeseburgers immediately while warm, accompanied by your favorite sides or fries.
