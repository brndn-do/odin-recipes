Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice is a savory Korean dish that combines cooked rice with kimchi, a spicy fermented cabbage. The dish typically includes ingredients like diced vegetables, such as onions and carrots, along with garlic, soy sauce, and often a protein like diced chicken or pork. The kimchi adds a tangy, slightly spicy flavor to the rice, while the stir-frying process caramelizes the ingredients, creating a satisfyingly aromatic and hearty meal. It's often garnished with sliced green onions and sesame seeds for added texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice
  • Kimchi
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Protein of your choice
  • Scallions
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Cooking oil of your choice

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Dice vegetables, mince garlic, chop kimchi, and slice scallions.
  2. Heat Cooking Oil: In a large skillet or wok, heat cooking oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Saute Vegetables and Protein: Add diced vegetables to the skillet and saut√© until softened. Add protein and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add Garlic and Kimchi: Add minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 30 seconds. Stir in chopped kimchi and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add Rice: Add cooked white rice to the skillet, breaking up clumps with a spatula.
  6. Season with Soy Sauce and Sesame Oil: Drizzle soy sauce and sesame oil over the rice. Stir well to combine.
  7. Finish and Garnish: Cook until rice is heated through and slightly crispy. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with scallions.
  8. Serve: Transfer to plates or bowls. Optionally garnish with sesame seeds.
